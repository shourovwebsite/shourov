Hello, I am shourov hasan, a Bangladesh-based freelance writer, editor, content strategist, and Virtual Assistant. I always produce content about creative people, research products, and ideas that stimulate meaningful changes in a variety of areas, from humanities and society to well-being and remedy. I am especially appareled in writing about the technology of the people and planet.\u00a0<\/p>

My specialist background is in the government and not-for-profit sectors, where I cultivate not only a high-level understanding of powerful communications but also admirable standards of professionalism. I am a professional writer; I express clearly, and I always love to provide on-time my best services and on budget negotiated.<\/p>

My concentration is to support you and develop your business or your clients\u2019 businesses with strong copy and content! I will produce attractive and informative blogs that will push traffic to your clients\u2019 sites and web copy that transform website visitors into a genuine customer. I understand how to craft the flawless post of link building and I have ghost-written hundreds of articles in my list that have been published on very high domain authority websites.<\/p>

l am majoring in English at National University. I frequently contribute articles about career planning programs and guiding professors, scholars, and alumni to many university publications. I have also written articles for popular monthly Bangla juvenile magazines such as Kishor alo for children and the art club at my college.<\/p>

I will research your specific topic and create an informative, SEO-friendly article under your requirements. I will work with you to build content that meets your requirements and helps your business goals.<\/p>\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
